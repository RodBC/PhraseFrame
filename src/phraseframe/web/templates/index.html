What the research actually says

RSVP removes natural eye movements, including previewing upcoming words and rereading confusing material. It can be useful for focus and controlled pacing, but it is not a shortcut around comprehension. For demanding texts, pause, step back, and use a lower pace.

Rayner et al. (2016) · Schotter et al. (2014) · Juola et al. (1995)
